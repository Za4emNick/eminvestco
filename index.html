About Eminvestco

American private company in global commodities

Eminvestco operates as a trading partner and coordinator between producers and buyers. We structure deals, organize delivery and manage the practical side of cross-border movement of fuels, raw materials, minerals, equipment and food.

  • Fuels & energy products
    Supply of diesel, jet fuel and other fuels with attention to specifications, quality and transport conditions.
  • Minerals & raw materials
    Delivery of minerals, ores and industrial raw materials to plants, processing facilities and end users.
  • Equipment & machinery
    Sourcing and exporting equipment for energy, infrastructure and industrial projects.
  • Food & agricultural products
    Supply of food and agricultural commodities for distributors and partners.
  • Import & export operations
    Coordination of documents, logistics, shipping and communication between sides in international trade.
